Digital Art Crew is a multimedia and tech education training company designed to teach individuals the fundamentals of design and computer programming through an online e-learning video platform.

Ready to get started?

Our Program

Our program is an affordable, 12-week course designed to get you prepared to land an entry-level position in the field of digital art and technology. Courses can be taken as individual modules or complete the entire program. By the end of the 8 week course you will have completed a personal website, professional resume and completed an interactive project.
