Our Program

Our program is an affordable, 8-week course designed to get you prepared to land an entry-level position in the field of digital art and technology. Courses can be taken as individual modules or complete the entire program. By the end of the 8 week course you will have completed a personal website, professional resume and completed an interactive project.
